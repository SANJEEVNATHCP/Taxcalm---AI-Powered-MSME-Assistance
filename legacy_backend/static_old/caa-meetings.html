📋 CAA Meeting Guidelines

Meeting Preparation

  • Prepare all relevant documents before the meeting
  • Ensure stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps recommended)
  • Test your audio and video 5 minutes before the meeting
  • Join from a quiet location with proper lighting
  • Have reference materials readily available

Meeting Conduct

  • Be punctual - join 5 minutes before scheduled time
  • Mute background noise when not speaking
  • Use professional language and dress code
  • Avoid multitasking during the meeting
  • Take notes for compliance and follow-up

Documentation

  • Meetings are automatically recorded for compliance
  • Ensure attendee list is complete and accurate
  • Document action items and responsibility owners
  • Send meeting minutes within 48 hours
  • Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information

Technical Support

  • If unable to connect, contact organizer immediately
  • Refresh your browser if experiencing issues
  • For recurring issues, clear browser cache
  • Use alternative devices if primary device fails
📌 Compliance Note: All CAA meetings are logged for audit trail purposes. Meeting recordings and attendee lists are securely stored in accordance with professional standards.
