📅 Zoom Meeting Scheduler

Schedule meetings with AI-powered natural language

📌 Just describe your meeting in natural language and let AI schedule it for you!
Use natural language like: "Meeting tomorrow at 3 PM", "Standup next Monday at 10 AM for 1 hour"

📚 Example Inputs

Simple meeting:
"Schedule a meeting tomorrow at 3 PM for 1 hour"
With topic:
"Create a team standup tomorrow at 10 AM for 30 minutes"
Specific day:
"Schedule a project review meeting next Friday at 2:30 PM for 2 hours"
Today's meeting:
"Book a meeting today at 4 PM for 45 minutes"
With custom title:
"Meeting called 'Budget Discussion' tomorrow at 11 AM for 1 hour"

💡 Tips

  • Always mention the time (hour and optional minutes)
  • Specify when (tomorrow, today, next Monday, Friday, etc.)
  • Duration defaults to 1 hour if not specified
  • You can use 12-hour (3 PM) or 24-hour format (15:00)
  • Add "for X hours/minutes" to set duration
